How to deal with the three meals when climbing?
Breakfast and dinner will be prepared by us. As for lunch, because most of the food is eaten on the road, it is necessary for individuals to prepare portable mobile food, such as bread, biscuits, and dried rice. There is no specific lunch time during the climbing process, (that is, it is not necessarily "feeding" at 12 o'clock), because climbing is a long-term physical expenditure. As long as you stop and rest, you need to eat and supplement at any time.

How much volume of drinking water do I need to bring?
The amount of water you drink varies from person to person. In terms of hiking for 6 to 8 hours a day, it is recommended that everyone prepare at least 1,000cc of drinking water. If you have a high demand for drinking water, you can prepare up to 1,500cc.

Can I wash in the mountains?
The lack of resources in high mountains, including water resources is the same. If the water source is quite sufficient, you can wipe your body with a turban slightly moistened, but it is impossible to take a shower. In addition, due to the low night temperature on the mountain, you must pay special attention when wiping your body to avoid catching cold.

What is AMS? How do I know if I have AMS?
Mountain sickness is a pathological condition caused by the decrease of oxygen concentration in high mountains. As long as the altitude exceeds 2,500m, headaches, nausea, rapid heartbeat, insomnia, vomiting, etc. can occur, and it can almost be concluded that it is altitude sickness.

The equipment is so complicated, it feels like it will cost a lot of money to buy it, can I rent it?
It is true that good mountaineering equipment is not cheap, but in fact, there is no need to rush to buy it. We all provide equipment rental services. For people who are climbing for the first time, they are usually not sure if they will fall in love with this activity and don’t know their needs well. You can rent it to see if you are afraid of the cold or heat, how many liters of backpack you can carry, and what trekking poles you need, and you can buy it later.

Is it better to wear rain boots or hiking shoes?
Both have their own hobbies and supporters. he comfort and protection of rain boots are not as good as hiking shoes, but the price is more affordable. However, because the rain boots are not covered enough and the sole thickness is not enough, it is easy to cause ankle sprain, toe pain and other conditions. A certain degree of experience is required, plus a little modification, before it is suitable for wearing the mountain. Therefore, for first-time climbers, it is best to wear hiking shoes.
